## Theme and setting

### Locations

Contemporary modern day, quiet medium sized but relatively wealthy, semi modern
town with a big animal population, especially homeless ones or ones in shelters.

### Lore

As tensions between animals and people supposed to take care of them the animals
decide to come together and take the fod and their required necessities for
themselves. Over time the movement grows to a city wide conflict for food and
acceptable treatment.

The game is supposed to in a casual way and with a colorful design showcase the
struggles of homeless animals and showcase how a collective resistance can make
a big improvement

Main plot consists of missions escalating the animal resistance and side
missions would refer to smaller tasks requested by other members of the group

### Characters

- Bodega cats
- Dogs
- other animals
- people representing corporations mostly shown as silhouettes without faces or
  faces covered by logos

## Gameplay & mechanics

### Goals challenges and quests

- the game consists of various levels
- the player chooses a set of heroes from the acquired ones
- some levels come with constraints
- quests require usage of specific heroes / timing or score to complete a level

### Interaction, controllers

- as a setup the player assembles a team and orders it
- the player chooses appropriate heroes and upgrades / skills for each mission
- as the party is progressing through the level the user can use keys and other
  controls to cast spells to defeat enemies
